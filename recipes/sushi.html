Sushi

Sushi is a popular dish originating from Japan

Ingredients

  • Raw Salmon
  • Seaweed/Nori
  • Rice
  • Rice vinegar

Instructions

  1. Pat down rice flat.
  2. Drizzle rice vinegar over rice.
  3. Place raw salmon cut in middle of rice.
  4. Place seaweed over rice and roll up Sushi.
  5. Cut up Sushi into desirable size.
