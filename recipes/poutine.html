Poutine

Poutine is the national dish of Canada. It can be described as fries and cheese curds topped off with gravy and any toppings wanted by the customer.

Ingredients

  • fries
  • gravy
  • Cheese curds

Instructions

  1. Prepare the fries on to a plate.
  2. Distribute the cheese curds on top of the fries.
  3. Drizzle the gravy on top of the dish.
